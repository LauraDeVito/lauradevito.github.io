Environmental Policy

Research Series

who shapes the response to environmental challenges, and why does the gap between ambition and outcomes persist?

A research series on environmental policy and governance: how challenges are framed, how responses are designed, and why the gap between ambition and outcomes persists. Aimed at policymakers, local authorities and civil society organisations engaged with environmental governance.

The Series

This page hosts short-form, non-peer-reviewed outputs, policy briefs and practical toolkits, developed alongside ongoing research into environmental policy and governance. The series covers three interconnected themes. Content is added as briefs are finalised. Get in touch via LinkedIn if you would like early access to a specific piece of work.

Just Transition

Outputs on how environmental transitions can be made fair and inclusive for workers, communities and places most affected by decarbonisation, with a focus on place-based industrial change and community voice in policy design.
