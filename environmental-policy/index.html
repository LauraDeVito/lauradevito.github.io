Environmental Policy

Research Series

who shapes the response to environmental challenges, and why does the gap between ambition and outcomes persist?

This series brings together policy briefs and practical outputs on just transition, nature-based solutions and climate adaptation. It sits outside the peer-review process, aimed at practitioners, local authorities and communities navigating the gap between environmental policy commitments and what is actually deliverable on the ground.
