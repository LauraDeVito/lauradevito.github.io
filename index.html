Dr Laura De Vito

Senior Research Fellow in Environmental Policy

I am a political scientist working on environmental policy and public administration, with two connected strands of research: how EU and national governments implement environmental policy, and how strategic foresight can support longer-term, more resilient policymaking.

I'm a Senior Research Fellow at the Centre for Environment, Society and Resilience, UWE Bristol. My current and recent work includes an ESRC Policy Fellowship with the Welsh Government on sustainable futures, research on air quality and citizen science, place-based industrial decarbonisation and just transition, nature-based solutions and advisory roles with the Bristol Advisory Committee on Climate Change and I chair the Scientific Board of Foundation Italian Futures Institute.
