Dr Laura De Vito

Senior Research Fellow in Environmental Policy

I am a Senior Research Fellow at the Centre for Environment, Society and Resilience, UWE Bristol. I have developed two connected strands of research: how EU, national, and local governments implement enviornmental policy, and how strategic foresight can support longer-term, more resilient policymaking.

My current and recent work includes an ESRC Policy Fellowship with the Welsh Government on sustainable futures, research on air quality and citizen science, place-based industrial decarbonisation and just transition, nature-based solutions, and advisory roles with the Bristol Advisory Committee on Climate Change. I chair the Scientific Board of the Italian Institute for the Future (IIF) Foundation.
