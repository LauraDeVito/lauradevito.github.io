Strategic Foresight

Research Series

what happens to foresight once it leaves the workshop room?

A research series on how strategic foresight methods are used, adapted and embedded in government. Governments face a structural tension between the short cycles of political life and the long timescales of the challenges they must address. Strategic foresight offers tools and frameworks for navigating that tension. This series translates ongoing research on foresight in public policy into accessible outputs for those seeking to embed longer-term thinking in policy design and institutional practice.
